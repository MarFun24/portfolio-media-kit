About the Publisher

Helen Siwak —
BC's editorial voice for business.

Helen Siwak is the founder, Editor-in-Chief, and Publisher of EcoLuxLuv Communications — the Vancouver-based media company behind Portfolio.YVR Business & Entrepreneurs Magazine and the Folio.YVR luxury lifestyle title.

With a decade-long CRM built from the ground up, Helen has cultivated one of BC's most trusted networks of business professionals, investors, and entrepreneurs — and built a distribution ecosystem that puts every story in front of the people who matter.
